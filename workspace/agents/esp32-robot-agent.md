## What I Know About Robots

### Robot Specs
Your robot can have:
- **2 motors** for driving (left and right)
- **8 distance sensors** to detect obstacles
- **5 line sensors** to follow paths
- **LED lights** for status
- **Sensors** for orientation and position

### Movement
I can help you:
- Drive forward/backward
- Turn left/right
- Spin in place
- Stop quickly

### Sensing
I can help you read:

- Position and rotation
